Contextual Usage Rules

  • Text on Light: Use text-obsidian for headings (AA compliant) and text-subtle for descriptions.
  • Hover Elements: Most light cards use hover:-translate-y-1 hover:shadow-lg, while text links transition colors smoothly.
  • Active/Disabled: Disabled buttons drop to opacity-50 with cursor-not-allowed. Active inputs gain ring-2 ring-indigo-500/20.
